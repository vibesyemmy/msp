How it works
Receive 50% reward on all donations

24/7 customer care service available on your dashboard

1. Mobile Number Verification

A unique security code is sent to your mobile number to verify your identity.

2. Matching

As a verified member, you will be matched to provide help to two other verified members just like you. This means that you will have to share the money into two equal parts and then send each part to one of the members. For instance, if you choose the basic package (₦20,000) you will have to pay #10,000 to one member and another ₦10,000 to the second member

3. Confirmation

You will then be required to upload the proof of your donation to each of the members so that they can confirm the receipt of your donation.

4. Donation and Benefit

As soon as your donations have been confirmed by the recipients, you will be eligible to receive three donations from 3 different members just like you. And you will be matched to receive anytime between 24hrs to 1 week.
